Orthopedic Services We Provide

When should I visit an orthopedic doctor for pain?

If pain persists more than a week or limits daily activities, book a consultation for diagnosis.

Can long-term knee pain be cured?

Many knee problems respond well to targeted treatment and physiotherapy; some degenerative conditions are managed to reduce symptoms.

Is back pain due to posture treatable?

Yes — posture-related pain often improves with exercise, postural training and targeted care.

Do you treat senior citizens & arthritis patients?

Yes. We offer tailored treatment plans for older patients focused on safety and function.

Can I avoid surgery with early treatment?

Many conditions can be treated conservatively if identified early; surgery is recommended only when necessary.

Do you offer emergency fracture treatment?

We provide initial fracture care and immobilization; complex cases are referred to specialist centres when required.
